What is Invader Zim?

Zim is a meme-coin category cryptocurrency inspired by the popular animated series Invader Zim (Invader Zim). This iconic animated series from Nickelodeon, created by Jhonen Vasquez, is known for its unique blend of black humor, science fiction, and satirical look at human society.
Memes like Zim are often created not only as digital assets, but also as part of internet culture. They attract attention through recognizable themes or characters, uniting a community of fans around an idea.
